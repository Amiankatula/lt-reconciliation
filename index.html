What LT-Reconciliation Does
🔄
Automatic Matching
Upload two CSV files — one from your mobile system, one from the bank — and the platform instantly compares every transaction.
⚠️
Error Categorisation
Every problem is categorised — Amount Mismatch, Missing Transaction, Timing Issue, or Possible Duplicate — so your team knows exactly what to investigate.
〰
Tolerance Matching
Set a KES tolerance threshold. Small acceptable differences are flagged separately so your team can focus on real problems.
🤖
Claude AI Analysis
After every reconciliation, Claude reads your results and gives a plain English explanation of what went wrong and what to do next.
🔧
AI Fix Suggestions
Claude generates a specific, actionable fix for every mismatch and missing transaction — so your operations team moves fast.
💬
Ask Claude Anything
Chat directly with Claude about your reconciliation results. Ask why something went wrong, what patterns exist, or what to escalate.
🕐
Full Run History
Every reconciliation run is saved with a full snapshot. Click any past run to reload the exact results — your complete audit trail.
⬇
CSV Export
Export your reconciliation results to a clean CSV file — ready to share with your manager, auditor, or banking partner.
How It Works
1
Upload your records — drop in your M-Pesa CSV and your core banking CSV. Or use our sample data to try it instantly.
2
Set your tolerance — choose a KES threshold for acceptable differences. Zero means strict matching.
3
Run reconciliation — the platform compares every transaction in seconds and categorises every result.
4
Get AI insights — Claude analyses your results, suggests fixes, and answers your questions in plain English.
5
Export and act — download your report as CSV, share with your team, and resolve issues fast.
